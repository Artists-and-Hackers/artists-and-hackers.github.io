Artists and Hackers
A Podcast On Creative Tools
Artists and Hackers is a podcast dedicated to the community building and using new digital tools of creation. We talk with programmers, artists, poets, musicians, bot-makers, educators, students and designers. We're looking at the current palette of artmaking online and take a critical eye to the history of technology and the internet. We're interested in where we've been and speculative ideas on its future.
